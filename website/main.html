Agincourt Reef

The Agincourt ribbon reefs are a small group of reefs running parallel to the Continental Shelf. Adjacent to the Coral Sea trench, these ribbon reefs are recognised as the most pristine eco-systems in the reef’s environment.

Flynn Reef

Flynn Reef is a coral reef found 20km east of Green Island in the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park. It is located on the Outer Reef and is about 60km offshore from Cairns.

Heart shaped

Hardy Reef in the Great Barrier Reef a heart-shaped mini atoll located about 100 km (60 mi) off the coast of Queensland and the Whitsunday Islands

Green island

Just 45 minutes from Cairns by fast catamarain, Green Island is a beautiful 6000 year old rainforest covered coral cay, located within the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park – one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World.
